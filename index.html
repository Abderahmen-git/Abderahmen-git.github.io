How I work

An incident response mindset, applied everywhere

Whether it's a live SOC alert or a misconfigured Kubernetes pod, the same loop applies: detect early, investigate with evidence, respond decisively, and feed what you learned back into detection.

01 · DETECT

Instrument the environment

Ship logs to the SIEM, write Sigma rules, and tune sources so real signal isn't buried in noise.

02 · INVESTIGATE

Establish ground truth

Pivot across TheHive, Cortex, and MISP to scope impact before drawing conclusions.

03 · RESPOND

Contain, then remediate

Move fast on containment, then fix the underlying gap — not just the symptom.

04 · IMPROVE

Close the loop

Turn every incident into a new detection rule, a hardened pipeline, or a documented playbook.
